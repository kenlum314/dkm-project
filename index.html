Introduction

This project came about as the members met and realized their fantastic background in new technology, could really help a BCIT teacher rebrand a course website he had been perfecting. All members attended the same class where the teacher(Jeff Parker) taught them about web development using HTML/5, CSS/3, jQuery and other related technology. However one day they were discussing with Jeff how out of date his course web site was, Jeff who was rather startled by the suggestion (but not insulted), quickly mentioned that he was planning on making their final assignment one that would could potentially completely replace the current website. In fact Jeff offered a monetary reward to any team of students who could create a better website that met ALL his criteria for improvement. That is how Project DKM came about rebranding the “COMP 1950 Intermediate Web Design and Development” website, coming in first place, passing the course with flying colors, collecting the prize money, and forever basking in the limelight of BCIT web developers everywhere.
